How to use google analytics?

This is not a step by step guide for anything specific. I will however, post many links and resources to help you better understand Google Analytics. The first thing you will want to do is make sure that you have all of your google ducks in a row. Google plays favorites and if you play by their rules you are bound to be successful.



If you have a client and are managing their website, make sure you have the following organized and fully synced.

  • Google Webmasters
  • Google MyBusiness
  • Google Analytics

    • It is very important to have everything from your website updated with google webmaster tools. Checkout their youtube channel and you can see for yourself. They also offer great advice about search engine optimization (SEO) and how to ensure you are doing 100% for your clients. I think a lot of people really try and rely too much on outside sources for their information. Google gives you all the answers, you just have to listen to what they are telling you.



    Get your certification

    Take their free classes

    You heard me right, FREE, the best four letter word besides love, f$%#, s&%@, c&%! and cake. Google Analytics offers completely free courses so you can learn how to use their programs. Check them out here and you can start learning how Google anlytics can actually help your business.



    I would also encourage your clients to download the Google MyBusiness application for their phone so they can see how programs such as Google Maps and Google Search play a major role in their business. Also, if you want a good selling point for a new client, speak a search query into the phone and see how they rank, if at all. If you can show ranking for an existing client to a new potential client, the sale will go much easier. Start small, keep educating yourself and you will be fine.

    The classes are short and feature youtube videos for every section.
